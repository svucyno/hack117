About AgriPredict

Agriculture meets
Artificial Intelligence

AgriPredict is an AI-powered crop intelligence platform built for farmers, agronomists, and researchers. We fuse satellite imagery, real-time weather, and soil science into one predictive engine — making precision agriculture accessible to every farm, everywhere.

AgriPredict works offline for core features — your last prediction, farm data, and recommendations are cached on your device. Live weather and satellite data require a brief internet connection once every 24 hours, making it usable even in low-connectivity rural areas.
Our current models achieve 91% accuracy averaged across 12 crop types, validated on held-out test data from real farms. Accuracy varies by crop and region — rice and wheat predictions in South Asia are above 94%, while newer crop models like quinoa are closer to 83% as we collect more training data.
One-time setup (3 minutes): farm location, area, soil type, and crop type. We auto-fetch weather and satellite data. You optionally enter soil test results (N, P, K, pH) for higher accuracy — but even without them, the system uses regional soil databases as a baseline.
Yes. Your farm data is encrypted at rest and in transit (AES-256, HTTPS). We never sell individual farm data to third parties. Anonymized, aggregated data may be used to improve model accuracy — you can opt out in settings. Researchers only see data with explicit farmer consent.
AgriBot is powered by GPT-4o with a custom system prompt that includes your farm's specific data: current crop, soil profile, location, and today's weather. This means answers are personalized to your farm — not generic internet advice. Every conversation is encrypted and not used to train OpenAI models.
AgriPredict offers a free tier with 5 predictions/month and basic weather data. The Farmer plan (₹299/mo) includes unlimited predictions, chatbot access, and satellite monitoring. The Researcher plan (₹999/mo) adds multi-farm analytics, historical data export, and API access.
